費用︱Fees

客戶需支付所有銀行費用（包括但不限於電匯費用），此類費用將從客戶的交易賬戶中扣除。使用銀聯人民幣存取款不收任何費用。

The client shall bear all banking charges (including but not limited to telegraphic transfer fees) and such fees will be deducted from the customer’s trading account. However, there is no charge for using Union Pay to deposit or withdraw money.

Leverage and margin requirement

槓桿和保證金要求

最低保證金要求大約是1%，1標準手黃金需要開倉保證金為美元1,000，而一標準手白銀的開倉保證金為美元1000。
在任何情況下，交易者都需要有足夠保證金維持開倉頭寸。文傳國際不提供追加保證金的通知。平倉按照以下規則執行：當客戶賬戶中維持頭寸的低於佔用保證金20%，所有開倉頭寸都會被自動平倉。根據開倉頭寸大小和未實現盈虧， 客戶將承受所有開倉頭寸被強制平倉以達到最低保證金要求的風險，且損失可能大於最先要求的保證金數額。

The minimum margin requirement is about 1%, with the initial margin of standard gold is $1,000 and the initial margin of standard silver is $1,000.

In any case, the trader needs to have enough margin to maintain the open position. Wenchuan International does not provide a notice of additional margin. Closing is performed according to the following rules: all open positions will be automatically closed when the customer's account maintains a position below the maintenance margin of 20%. Depending on the size of the open position and unrealized profit and loss, the client will be at risk of all open positions being forced to close to the minimum margin requirement and the loss may be greater than the initial margin requirement.

隔夜利息︱The rollover interest

當客戶持有倉位，穿越北京時間上午 5:00（夏令時間）或上午6:00（冬令時間），客戶的倉位便會有收取或付出過夜利息的情況。各種產品的過夜利息會隨着息口差距而變化，過夜利息的詳情可在平台作詳細了解。於＂市場報價＂右擊，選擇＂商品列表＂，再選擇需查詢的產品，然後點擊屬性,在＂買單調期庫存費/賣單調期庫存費＂便可查看買/賣過夜利息。我們的過夜利息，會根據市場的利率，每星期調整一次，請到我們的交易平台查看當周利率。

周末與節假日

>跨周末頭寸的隔夜利息將會在星期三計算，所以，星期三的隔夜利息將是其他交易日的三倍。當被交易的產品遇到公眾假期或銀行休息，將會有假日的隔夜利息產生。假日隔夜利息通常會在假日前兩個工作日計算。隔夜利息報價可從MT4平台上查看到。請查看常見問題了解隔夜利息詳情。

When the customer holds a position, crossing the 5:00 a.m. (summer time) or 6:00 a.m. (winter time) of Beijing time, the customer’s position will be charged or paid rollover interest. The rollover interest of various products will vary with the interest gap. Details of the rollover interest can be found on the platform. Right click on “Market Quote”, select “Product List”, then select the product to be queried, and next click on the attribute, then you can view the buy/sell rollover interest rate in “Pay for Rescheduling Inventory/Sell Order Adjustment Inventory Fee”. And our rollover interest rate will be adjusted once a week which is according to the market interest rate. So please check our trading platform for the weekly interest rate.

Weekends and holidays

Rollover interest on positions across the weekend will be calculated on Wednesday, so the rollover interest on Wednesday will be three times higher than on other trading days. There will be rollover interest generated for the holiday when the product is traded encounters a public holiday or a bank break. The rollover interest on the holiday is usually calculated two business days before the holiday. And the rollover interest offer can be viewed on the MT4 platform. So please check frequently asked questions for rollover interest details.

交易確認︱Trade confirmations

當交易被執行後，將可在網上查看到交易確認。完整的交易詳情（包括日期、時間、價格、買入或賣出的倉位價值、美元價值和參考號）可通過交易平台上查看。

When the trade is executed, a confirmation of the transaction will be available online. And complete transaction details (including date, time, price, value of positions bought or sold, dollar value and reference number) can be viewed on the trading platform.

對沖交易︱Hedging transaction

通過文傳國際的MT4平台，您可以針對同一種交易產品建立和持有多個倉位和定單。當交易者對沖時，我們將會要求交易者就對沖的方向的持倉存放50%的保證金。對沖交易平倉時，只需一如其他交易一樣使用平倉、止損或止盈單即可。客戶必須個別地將每個買入及賣出持倉平倉。

With the MT4 platform of Wenchuan International, you can create and hold multiple positions and orders for the same trading product. When the trader hedges, we will ask the trader to deposit a 50% deposit on the position in the direction of the hedge. When a hedge trade is closed, you only need to close the position, stop loss or take profit order as you would any other trade. Customers must individually close each buy and sell position.
