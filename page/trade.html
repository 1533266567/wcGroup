什麼是異常交易︱What is abnormal trade?

異常交易意旨客戶於極短時間內進行密集式交易，總結行內經驗，短線交易最終對客戶並沒有任何益處，因為客戶須為頻密的交易付出巨大的手續費。同時短線交易亦非正確的投資方法，客戶無法在交易中獲得較大的利潤。另外，亦有不法之徒意圖利用異常交易進行洗黑錢活動，本公司為香港金銀業貿易場附屬公司，特別關注此等不法活動，任何有機會觸犯“防止洗黑錢”條例的異常交易，本公司是絕對不會容許的！

異常交易會大量佔用網絡資源，除了會影響交易系統的穩定性及效率，更會妨礙一般客戶的交易。為保障大部分客戶的利益情況下，本公司絕不能接受異常交易行為繼續發生。

Abnormal trade means that customers conduct intensive transactions in a very short time. To sum up the experience in the industry, short-term trading is not beneficial to the customer for the customer has to pay a huge handling fee for frequent transactions. At the same time, short-term trading is also an incorrect investment method, and customers cannot obtain large profits in the transaction. In addition, there are also criminals who intend to use abnormal trades for money laundering activities. We are a subsidiary company of membership in the Hong Kong Chinese Gold and Silver Exchange and we are particularly concerned about these illegal activities. Any opportunity to violate the "prevention of money laundering" regulation of abnormal trades, the company will not tolerate!

Abnormal trade takes up a lot of network resources. Moreover, in addition to affecting the stability and efficiency of the trading system, it will hinder the trading of ordinary customers. In order to protect the interests of the majority of customers, the company must not accept the Abnormal trade continues to occur.

異常交易的定義︱The definition of abnormal trade

當客戶取款時，我們會審核客戶上次取款（首次取款則從開戶開始）至本次取款的所有交易，按照交易量統計，當交易單中有50% 交易量的持倉時間低於5分鐘時，我們就會對此賬戶進行進一步審核，審核時間需要延長3小時。

When the customer withdraws money, we will review the customer’s all transactions from the last withdrawal (the first withdrawal starts from the account opening) to this time. According to the transaction volume, when the 50% trading volume in the trading order has a holding time of less than 5 minutes, we will conduct further review of this account, and the review time needs to be extended by 3 hours.

1.当疑似异常交易的交易单中有30%交易量的持仓时间低于5分钟时。

When there is a 30% trading volume in a transaction record suspected of abnormal trade, the holding time is less than 5 minutes.

2.当疑似异常交易的交易单中有30%交易量属于5分钟内建立的对冲锁仓单。

In the transaction record suspected of abnormal trade, 30% of the trading volume belongs to the hedged lock position orders established within 5 minutes.

3.客户使用同一账户并同时利用多台计算器终端机进行交易行为，意图以＂密集式交易＂手段令市场造成巨大成交额的误导信息。

The act of a customer using software vulnerabilities to trade with the assistance of third-party software (referred as the "plug-in software"). (please contact online customer service staff if you need to use automatic trading.)

4.客户借助第三方软件（即＂外挂软件＂）利用软件漏洞进行交易的行为。（如需使用自动代程序交易，请联系在线客服人员）

The act of a customer using software vulnerabilities to trade with the assistance of third-party software (referred as the "plug-in software"). (please contact online customer service staff if you need to use automatic trading.)

5.利用互联网或计算器＂报价延误＂，＂多次＂故意或＂恶意＂在5分钟之内＂重仓＂进出，并且在短时间之内赚取交易平台非市场价格的价差的一种交易。

A trade of utilizing the internet or computers to conduct "quote delay", and repeatedly "intentionally" or "maliciously" trading in and out of market with "heavy positions" within five minutes, that earns a spread on the trading platform rather than the market price in a short period of time.

6.当疑似异常交易的交易数量跟过往的存在甚大差异，如瞬间由0.1至0.5手转变为5至10手交易。

When the number of trades suspected to be abnormal is greatly different from the past, for example, the trading volume changes from 0.1- 0.5 to 5 - 10 in an instant.

處理方法︱Handling method

1.如經本公司調查後，發現帳號在進行異常交易，本公司會馬上凍結該帳號的交易和資金進出再作深入調查。

2.如確認該帳號在進行異常交易，本公司會把該帳號註銷，並把帳戶的剩餘資金髮還客戶，任何因異常交易所產生的利潤和傭金不發放。

＊異常交易的解釋權歸文傳國際有限公司所有，如有任何爭議，一切以文傳國際之決定為最終裁決。

1. If the account is confirmed to be in an abnormal transaction, the company will cancel the account and return the remaining funds of the account to the customer, and any profits and commissions arising from abnormal transactions.

2. If the account is confirmed to be in an unusual transaction, the company will cancel the account and return the remaining funds of the account to the customer, any profits and commissions arising from unusual transactions.

The right to interpret the abnormal transaction is owned by Wenchuan International Co., Ltd. and if there is any dispute, the decision of Wenchuan International shall be final.
