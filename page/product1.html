費用︱Fees

客戶需支付所有銀行費用（包括但不限於電匯費用），此類費用將從客戶的交易賬戶中扣除。使用銀聯人民幣存取款不收任何費用。

The client shall bear all banking charges (including but not limited to telegraphic transfer fees) and such fees will be deducted from the customer’s trading account. However, there is no charge for using Union Pay to deposit or withdraw money.
