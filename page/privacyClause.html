隐私条款︱Privacy Policy

文傳重視個人隱私權而且非常重視與客戶的關係，因此文傳會根據香港《個人資料（私隱）條例》（下稱「條例」）之規定，力求保護客戶權益。當客戶向文傳申請一個真實或模擬賬戶時，文傳會收集相關個人資料。同時，文傳可能使用客戶的姓名、電話號碼、電郵地址及通訊地址作直接促銷投資產品和服務以提升服務。

客戶的個人資料按實際情況和需要會被用作以下用途：
1. 執行客戶指示或回復客戶有關的查詢的核證程序、賬戶的日常行政管理或相關投資產品或服務的直接或間接市場推廣，與文傳的聯屬或關連公司就相關投資或交易服務共享、反覆查證及轉移該等個人資料；

2. 處理客戶抵押品，或向追收客戶欠款；

3. 將該等個人資料轉移予第三者服務供應商作信貸查證及／或核證數據用途；

4. 關於或有關遵守任何法律、規例、法令或監管機構的命令的任何用途而提供任何該等數據；

5. 關於或有關文傳或文傳的聯屬或關連公司的投資業務的直接或間接市場推廣。該等數據可能會被轉移至香港以外的地方。

文傳會把客戶的個人資料保密，但按實際情況和需要可能提供給下列機構／人士：
1. 文傳其他公司包括全資擁有的附屬公司或附屬公司的聯營公司或集團屬下的董事、職員等名義的全資或部份擁有的公司；

2. 為客戶處理相關業務的代理人及第三方服務供應商包括：結算所、專業服務公司、金融機構、電腦系統服務商、電訊系統支持服務，作為完成客戶服務的必要手續；

3. 有關或監管文傳任何業務的任何監管或政府機構；

4.根據保密責任文傳視為適當或合適的人士，包括集團的成員公司及其僱員。這些授權人士必須把客戶的個人資料保密。

根據條例中的條款，客戶均有權要求文傳更正有關客戶的任何不實數據及以書面方式要求我們終止使用其個人資料作直接或間接市場推廣用途。唯文傳有權就處理根據條例中的條款而提出索取或更正數據的要求而收取合理的費用。

任何查詢或申請有關其個人資料，可填妥由香港個人資料（隱私）專員提供的「查閱數據要求表格」，將填妥的表格連同客戶簽署確認的身份證明文件副本致函文傳註冊辦事處之法律及合規部。

Wenchuan values personal privacy and attaches great importance to its relationships with customers. Therefore, the Wenchuan will strive to protect the interests of customers in accordance with the requirements of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (hereinafter referred to as the “Ordinance”). When a client applies for a real or simulated account to Wenchuan, the company will collect relevant data about the applier. At the same time, Wenchuan may use the customer’s name, phone number, email address and correspondence address to directly promote investment products and services to enhance services.

The customer’s personal data will be used for the following purposes according to actual situation and needs:
1. Verify procedures, daily administrative management of accounts, direct or indirect marketing that are related to investment products or services that perform customer instructions or respond to customer inquiries; and share, repeatedly verify and transfer such personal data with related affiliates or related companies of Wenchuan on relevant investment or transaction services.

2. Handle customer collateral or recover the debt owed by the customer;

3. Transfer such personal data to the third-party service providers for credit verification and/or data verification purposes;

4. Provide any such data with respect to or related to comply with any law, regulation, decree or order of the regulator;

5. Direct or indirect marketing of investment business of affiliated or related companies of Wenchuan, or is related to Wenchuan. Such information may be transferred to locations outside Hong Kong.

The Wenchuan will keep the customer’s personal information confidential, but may provide it to the following organizations and persons according to the actual situation and needs:
1. Other companies of Wenchuan, including wholly-owned subsidiaries, or affiliates of subsidiaries, or wholly-owned or partially owned companies in the name of directors and staff of the Group;

2. The agents and the third-party service providers handling relevant business for customers, including clearing houses, professional service companies, financial institutions, computer system service providers, telecommunications system support services as necessary procedures for completing customer service;

3. Any regulatory or government agency relating to or regulating any business of Wenchuan;

4. The appropriate or qualified personnel who is regarded by the Wenchuan under the confidentiality obligation, including members of the group and its employees. And these authorized persons must keep the customer's personal information confidential.

According to the terms of the ordinance, the customers shall have the right to request Wenchuan to correct false data relating to the customers and to terminate the use of his personal data for direct or indirect marketing purposes in writing. Only Wenchuan has the right to charge a reasonable fee for handling requests for correcting data in accordance with the terms of the Ordinance.

Any inquiries or applications relating to their personal data may be completed by the “Review Data Request Form” provided by the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Commissioner. The completed form together with the copy of identity document signed by the customer will be sent to the Legal and Compliance Department of the registered office.
